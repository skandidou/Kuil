Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Summary: Kuil helps you create LinkedIn content using AI. We collect only what's necessary to provide this service, and we never sell your data.

1. Introduction

Kuil ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information from LinkedIn

When you connect your LinkedIn account, we access:

  • Profile Information: Name, email, profile picture, headline
  • Posts: Your recent LinkedIn posts (for voice analysis)
  • Publishing Access: Permission to publish posts on your behalf

2.2 Information You Provide

  • Content you create or edit within the app
  • Your professional role and communication preferences
  • Feedback and support requests

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage analytics (features used, session duration)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Error logs for troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Analyze your writing style to generate personalized content
  • Publish posts to LinkedIn on your behalf when you request it
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Send important service updates
  • Provide customer support

4. AI Processing

We use Anthropic's Claude AI to:

  • Analyze your writing voice from existing posts
  • Generate content suggestions matching your style
  • Provide engagement predictions

Important: Your content is processed in real-time and not stored by our AI provider for training purposes.

5. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Strict access controls to sensitive data
  • Token Security: LinkedIn tokens are encrypted before storage
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Generated Content: Stored until you delete it or your account
  • Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized after 90 days

7. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Objection: Opt-out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, use the settings in the app or contact us.

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • LinkedIn: To publish content you approve
  • Anthropic (Claude): For AI content generation (not stored)
  • Service Providers: Hosting (Railway), Database (Supabase), Cache (Upstash)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

9. Children's Privacy

Kuil is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States and European Union. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:
